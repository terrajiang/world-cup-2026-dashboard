Group Stage

  • 48 teams are split into 12 groups of 4.
  • Each team plays the other 3 teams in its group once.
  • A win is 3 points, a draw is 1 point, and a loss is 0 points.
  • The top 2 teams in every group advance.
  • The 8 best third-place teams also advance.

Tiebreakers

  • Teams are ranked first by points.
  • If tied, goal difference is checked next.
  • If still tied, goals scored is checked next.
  • If teams are still level, head-to-head and fair-play rules can decide it.

Knockout Stage

  • 32 teams enter a single-elimination bracket.
  • If a knockout match is tied after 90 minutes, extra time is played.
  • If it is still tied after extra time, penalties decide the winner.
  • Winners move on; losers are out, except semifinal losers play the third-place game.
